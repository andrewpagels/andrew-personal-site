The future is created by those who care enough to create it.

I came into the University not knowing more than a modicum about what I wanted to do, and how I could add value to the world.

I leave, this Spring, with a clear picture of my own vector of impact, feeling empowered and confident. I have learned not a set of facts in my time here, but a mindset - a way of seeing the world as something that is constantly being edited, changed, improved by the people who care to do it. Now, I feel that I can be one of those people.

It's a start, at least.
